Fast3D Vertex Format

Format Breakdown

The 16 byte Fast3D vertex format contains five pieces of information:

  • The first three 16-bit shorts are the vertex positions in XYZ.
  • The next 16-bit short is the unused flag.
    • (Note: In the assembler display lists, flag is not represented since it is unused in every version of Fast3D).
  • The next two 16-bit shorts are the U and V texture position data.
  • the last four bytes are the vertex color data in RGBA form.

Together, these 16 bytes of information form one complete vertex.
